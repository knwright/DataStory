Sasquatch, Bigfoot, Yeti, Ape-Man, George...
Whatever you want to call this creature, it has been mysteriously roaming the world.

Little is known about it. It howls at night. It leaves footprints. It is always just out of focus in photographs.
So, is it real? Are people who have sightings just crackpots? Or, is there something out there?

Below is the Patterson-Gimlin film, one of the most famous Bigfoot sightings. Recorded in 1967 in California, the authenticity of the film has been a heated topic for decades.


As you can see, there have been thousands of sightings across the continental United States. From the looks of it, this creature tends to gravitate towards areas where they are able to find places to hide. Notice the desert and the plains states do not have nearly as many sightings.
